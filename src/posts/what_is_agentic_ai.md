# **Definition**
Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems designed to operate autonomously with a degree of self-direction, problem-solving, and decision-making capabilities. Their key strength lies in their ability to take initiative, adapt to changing environments, and work toward a desired goal with minimal human intervention.

# **Key Features**
### **Autonomous**
- They can operate autonomously with little to minimal input from humans.
### **Adaptability**
 - They can adapt to the changes in the environment and act accordingly to align themself with towards the desired end goal
###  **Decision-Making**
 - They can make their own decision and take action that is influenced by their desired end goal, and all this is happening without a human input.
###  **Interactive Capabilities**
 - They are able to communicate or collaborate with human or other agentic ai's to get the information they need while working towards the goal.

#  **Challenges**
- Since agentic Ai Operates with a high degree of autonomy, there is an important ethical and technical challenges to consider which may include:
	- **Losing Control** - How do we ensure that we prevent unintended behaviours and losing control over the agents
	-  **Bias and Fairness** - The are instances where fairness is required and being bias can have very large effects.
	- **Security** - How do we prevent people with malicious intent from connecting and communicating with the collaborative agents.
# **Real life applications**
### **Autonomous Vehicles**
- Agentic AI can integrate with traffic management systems and communicate with other autonomous agents on the road to make real-time decisions.
    
- It can analyse factors like weather conditions, road congestion, and pedestrian movement to optimize driving behaviour and ensure a safe journey.

### **Personal AI Assistants**
- Unlike traditional AI assistants that rely heavily on user commands, agentic AI proactively manages your digital environment.

- For example, it can monitor your calendar, detect that you have a meeting at location ABC, check your current location via maps, and automatically notify attendees if you're running late—without requiring user input.
### **Intelligent NPCs in Video Games**
- NPCs powered by agentic AI can make independent decisions, respond dynamically to player actions, and create a more immersive gaming experience.

### **And more....** 
- From industrial automation to smart home management, agentic AI is transforming multiple industries by enabling AI to act with greater independence and efficiency.
